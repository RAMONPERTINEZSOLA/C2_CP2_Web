‚úèÔ∏è Updating stock quantities

The most common action is to update the quantity of a product after taking items from the drawer or adding new ones.

  1. Open the correct drawer page.
  2. Find the row of the product you have used or added.
  3. Click on the quantity field.
  4. Type the new quantity (only numbers, no spaces).
  5. Click Save at the bottom or top of the table.
  6. Wait a few seconds and refresh the page once to confirm the change.
üîÅ Habit to remember: Update ‚Üí Save ‚Üí Refresh once. This keeps the screen in sync with what everyone else will see.
‚ö†Ô∏è Avoid using negative numbers or text in the quantity fields. If you are not sure about the correct number, leave the value as it is and ask a supervisor.

üì∏ Adding or changing drawer photos

Photos make it much easier to quickly recognise the content of a drawer. You can upload a new picture when:

  • The layout of the drawer has changed significantly.
  • The current photo is blurry, dark or outdated.
How to upload a new photo
  1. Open the correct drawer page.
  2. Find the photo section or current image.
  3. Use the Upload or Change photo button.
  4. Select the image from your device (preferably JPG, medium size).
  5. Wait until the upload finishes.
  6. Refresh the page after a short moment to see the new photo.
Good photo practices
  • Avoid very large files (photos around 0.7‚Äì1 MB are ideal).
  • Take the picture from the front, so all products are visible.
  • Do not photograph confidential documents or screens.
Use clear photos taken from the front to help everyone recognise the drawer.
üö´ If the photo upload fails, do not try again many times in a row. Check your Wi-Fi or network, and try once more with a smaller image.

üîó Using the QR labels on the drawers

Many drawers may have a QR sticker. Scanning this QR with your phone sends you directly to the correct drawer page in the web app.

How to use a QR
  1. Open the camera on your phone (or a QR app if needed).
  2. Point it at the QR label on the drawer.
  3. Tap the notification or link that appears.
  4. The drawer page will open in your browser.
üì± Using the QR labels is often the fastest way to open the correct page. It avoids typing the wrong rack or drawer code.

‚ùì Quick questions & answers (FAQ)

Do I need any special software to use this site?
No. Any modern browser is enough (Chrome, Edge, Safari, ‚Ä¶). You do not need Excel, GitHub or any other tool.
Who can see the changes I make?
Everyone who uses the warehouse site. The idea is that stock is shared and transparent, so the whole team can rely on the same information.
Can I undo a mistake myself?
You can manually correct a wrong quantity or description if you notice it immediately. For bigger errors, contact the person responsible for maintaining the data.
Can I add new products or drawers?
Normally, only supervisors or power users should add new drawers or completely new products. If you think something is missing, talk to them first.
What if I forget to update the quantity?
Please update it as soon as you remember. The closer we keep the system to reality, the easier it is for everyone to work smoothly.
